How We Work

1

Analysis

We give the utmost importance to understand and document clients' inputs, design, and branding preferences. We also clarify all the open-ended points to establish a precise and clear idea about both clients’ and project goals. We work in a collaborative approach involving key stakeholders to bring alignment in the business process.

2

Strategy

Our services emphasize on well-defined project planning and leave nothing for assumption to avoid miscommunication. We note down how we are going to proceed further, define detailed technical specifications, devise the design & branding guidelines of the project, than take confirmation and approval from the client end on the documents and materials.

3

Design

We provide clients' with Web, Tablet, and Mobile-specific UI designs of the projects based on the approved wireframes and design guidelines. As a trusted entity, we deliver clickable prototypes on invision platform and the source files in either Photoshop or Sketch format. Getting system UI designs approved and confirmed by the client is one of our objective at this stage.

4

Development

At this stage, we cater requirements for frontend, backend, web services, and API development integration. Along with preparing a strategy for agile scrum methodology, we factor the aspects of scalability, multi-tenancy, 3rd party integration, and craft an optimized clean code structure using cutting-edge technologies. We make sure to involve and implement clients' reviews in each sprint/milestone.

5

Launch

We test each sprint manually, report bugs and add those to product backlog. Once the bug fixing is done, we provide the quality release and then the final demo of the sprint is sent for clients’ approval. We make sure to do regression testing to ensure proper functioning of the previously approved milestones/splints.
